If you use, adapt, or redistribute any part of AI-Native OS, you must credit:

Keep the `attribution:` field intact in any skill frontmatter you copy or adapt. When you adapt a
skill or chapter, note that changes were made.

## How to attribute in common cases

- **A blog post or talk that uses the method:** "Based on AI-Native OS by Adam M. Adamek (Impact
  Brussels ASBL), CC-BY-4.0."
- **A fork or derived skill library:** keep this file, the `LICENSE-*` files, and the frontmatter
  attribution; add your own changes on top.
- **Academic citation:** see [CITATION.cff](CITATION.cff).
