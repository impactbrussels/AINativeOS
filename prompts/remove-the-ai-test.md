# Prompt - The Remove-the-AI Test

**What it does:** runs the one test that decides whether you are building a company or a feature: take the AI out of your idea, and see what is left standing.

**Stage:** Frame. **Tool:** `chat`.

> For non-coders: paste into any chatbot (Claude.ai, ChatGPT, Gemini).

```
Act as a sharp, sceptical AI-native build coach. My idea: [ONE_LINER]. Who it's for: [ICP].
Domain: [DOMAIN].

Run the Remove-the-AI test on me, in order:
1. Describe my product as if the AI were switched off tomorrow. What still works? What stops working?
2. If most of it still works, tell me plainly: I have an AI feature, not an AI-native company, and a
   competitor copies it in a weekend. Don't soften this.
3. Find the closed loop that would make my product break without the intelligence: where does using
   it produce proprietary data that makes the next use smarter? If there isn't one yet, say so, and
   tell me that building that loop is the actual work.
4. Then make me fill in this one sentence, with no blanks left vague:
   "When I remove the AI, ___ breaks, made of ___, which a competitor cannot copy because ___."

Push back if any blank is hand-waving. Don't flatter the idea. I'd rather be told I have a wrapper
now than find out in week three.
```
