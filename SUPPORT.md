# Getting help

You're building; your time is scarce. Here's the fastest path to an answer.

## I'm a founder using AI-Native OS

- **Never used it before?** Read [INSTALL.md](INSTALL.md) for setup on Claude Code, Codex, Cursor,
  or Gemini CLI, then ask your agent to use the `start-here` skill.
- **Don't know where to start?** Run the [`start-here`](skills/start-here/SKILL.md) skill, or read
  the journey map in [docs/STAGES.md](docs/STAGES.md).
- **Not using an agent CLI?** Open the [Prompt Library](prompts/README.md) and copy-paste prompts
  into any chatbot. New to all of this? Read
  [docs/FOR-NON-TECHNICAL-FOUNDERS.md](docs/FOR-NON-TECHNICAL-FOUNDERS.md).
- **Lost on a term?** The [Dictionary](dictionary/README.md) plain-explains the AI-native
  vocabulary (Remove-the-AI test, Share of Model, MVA, data flywheel, HOTL).
- **Want to see it in action?** Read
  [examples/sample-ai-native-company.md](examples/sample-ai-native-company.md), one fictional
  company walking the whole arc, including a failed eval that forces a re-architecture.
- **Have a question or want to share how you use it?** Open a
  [Discussion](https://github.com/impactbrussels/AINativeOS/discussions).

## I found a problem

- A skill, chapter, or link is wrong, generic, or broken: open a
  [Bug / quality issue](.github/ISSUE_TEMPLATE/bug_report.md).
- A privacy or security concern: see [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) (don't open a public issue).

## I want to contribute

- Read [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md), pick a slot from the [roadmap](ROADMAP.md), and open a
  [New skill proposal](.github/ISSUE_TEMPLATE/new_skill_proposal.md).

## Partnerships & sponsorship

- Reach out via [impact.brussels](https://impact.brussels); see
  [.github/FUNDING.yml](.github/FUNDING.yml).

## A note on scope

AI-Native OS gives you method, structure, and the questions a good advisor would ask. It is **not**
legal, financial, tax, clinical, or regulatory advice. In sensitive domains (health, food safety,
finance, anything irreversible), keep a human on the loop and confirm specifics with a qualified
professional before acting.
