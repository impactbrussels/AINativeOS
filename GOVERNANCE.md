# Governance

How AI-Native OS is run, so contributors know what to expect and the project can be trusted as a
durable source of truth.

## Stewardship

AI-Native OS is stewarded by **Impact Brussels ASBL** (a Belgian non-profit, BE 1033.129.479) and
maintained by **Adam M. Adamek**, the project's lead maintainer for now. As the contributor base
grows, maintainership will expand to a small team named in `CODEOWNERS`.

## Principles that don't change

These are the project's constitution. Changes to them require a maintainer decision and a
documented rationale in a PR:

1. **Theme-agnostic.** AI-Native OS serves every founder; no sector is privileged or baked in.
   Use the placeholder glossary (`[ICP]`, `[PROBLEM]`, `[DOMAIN]`).
2. **Proof before hype.** Skills and chapters cite sources, flag assumptions, and never invent
   facts, market numbers, or regulatory timelines. A demo is not evidence; a draft is not a
   decision. Contested figures carry `[claimed in source]` and live in `handbook/SOURCES.md`.
3. **Open and attribution-only.** Content stays CC-BY-4.0, code stays Apache-2.0. Attribution is
   always required.
4. **Funding never buys advice.** Support keeps the commons open; it never bends a skill's
   recommendation. (Funding via [impact.brussels](https://impact.brussels); see
   [.github/FUNDING.yml](.github/FUNDING.yml).)

## How decisions are made

- **Everyday changes** (new skills, fixes, docs): proposed via issue, built via PR, merged by a
  maintainer after review against [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md) and the PR checklist.
- **Roadmap changes**: anyone can propose a slot; maintainers curate [ROADMAP.md](ROADMAP.md) to
  keep it coherent and non-overlapping.
- **Significant changes** (structure, licensing, the principles above): opened as a Discussion or
  an issue labelled `rfc`, left open for community input, then decided by the maintainer with the
  rationale recorded. Voice and architecture decisions are logged in
  [docs/DECISIONS.md](docs/DECISIONS.md).

## Review standard

Every contribution is reviewed for: correctness, the quality bar in
[CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md), theme-agnosticism, real method in `references/`, the house
voice ([docs/VOICE-GUIDE.md](docs/VOICE-GUIDE.md)), and preserved attribution. Maintainers may
request changes, edit for the house voice, or decline contributions that don't meet the bar. A thin
or generic skill is worse than no skill.

## Becoming a maintainer

Consistent, high-quality contributions and good judgement in review earn an invitation to maintain.
Maintainers are expected to uphold the principles above and the
[CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).
