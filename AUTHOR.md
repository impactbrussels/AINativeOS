# The Author

**Adam M. Adamek, PhD**, Innovation Architect, founder of [Impact Brussels ASBL](https://impact.brussels).

Twenty-five years in food and biotech R&D (Merck, Danone, Mondelēz, MARS, EIT Food), working on
the science where a confident guess that skips the evidence costs millions: novel foods, EFSA
regulatory pathways, precision fermentation, alternative protein, strategic foresight. Then I crossed
the line from advising builders to being one, teaching myself to build in the open with AI as the
core, not the garnish.

AI-Native OS comes out of that crossing. I watched the same scientific discipline I'd spent a career
defending (evidence before excitement) collide with a new failure mode: founders shipping wrappers
and calling them AI companies. This repo is the method I wish I'd had: how to make intelligence
load-bearing, how to architect before you code, and how to build a moat out of data and trust in
sectors where the regulation is real.

## Why I write

I write to make hard-won method **runnable** and **open**. The lessons that decide whether a company
lives (validate before you build, make the model refute you, put a human on the loop before you act
on a person) arrive late, scattered, and behind paywalls. I'd rather encode them once, in the open,
so the next founder starts a year ahead instead of from zero.

AI-Native OS is a sibling to **[FounderOS](../FounderOS/)**: that one walks a first-time founder
through the startup journey; this one is for the founder building when the tools got powerful faster
than the wisdom did.
