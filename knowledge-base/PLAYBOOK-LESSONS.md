### 2026-06-19: A wrapper and a native product look identical in a demo; they diverge in week three.

**Context:** Seeding the OS, the recurring pattern behind the Remove-the-AI test.
**What happened:** Across the source case studies, the products that survived (NotCo, Nuritas,
OpenEvidence) all had a closed loop where usage produced proprietary data that improved the system;
the ones flagged as fragile relied on a single model call with no compounding asset behind it.
**Lesson:** Judge an AI product by what it accrues, not by what it demos. If nothing compounds with
use, the demo is the ceiling.
**Source:** AI-native startups synthesis (seed), Handbook ch.14.
