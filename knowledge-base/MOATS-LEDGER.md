# AI-Native OS: Moats Ledger

A living ledger of the founder's defensibility, because the moat is the whole point of building
AI-native. In the AI era the moat is almost never the model (everyone rents the same one); it is
the **proprietary data flywheel** and the **earned trust**. Grown by `capture-learning`; reviewed in
the `moat-strategy` skill. Append, never overwrite.

Track each moat candidate against four questions:

| Moat candidate | What compounds with use? | Can a horizontal AI giant reach this data? | What trust/regulatory asset protects it? | Strength (weak / forming / real) |
|----------------|--------------------------|--------------------------------------------|------------------------------------------|----------------------------------|
| *(example)* Proprietary peptide-bioactivity dataset | Each validated discovery sharpens the next prediction | No: generated by domain-specific wet-lab + genomics | Patent + validated provenance | forming |

## The four moat types in AI-native companies (seed)

1. **Data flywheel**: every interaction makes the product better, attracting more users and more
   data. The loop, not the snapshot, is the moat.
2. **Vertical proprietary data**: domain data a horizontal giant cannot collect (regulated,
   physical, relationship-gated). Collected by doing the unglamorous work.
3. **Trust and compliance**: in health/food/finance, the audit trail, validation record, and
   regulatory clearances a buyer requires before they'll act. Slow to build, slow to copy.
4. **Ecosystem and consortium position**: in fragmented sectors, the standardised shared dataset or
   the partner network you anchor. Defensible because it's collective.
